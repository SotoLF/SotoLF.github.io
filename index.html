About Me

I am a bioinformatician and researcher specializing in computational biology, with expertise in transcription factor analysis, vaccine design, and COVID-19 research. My work focuses on developing computational tools for biological data analysis and integrating multi-omics data to understand complex biological systems.

With a strong background in both biology and computer science, I apply computational approaches to solve complex biological problems, particularly in the areas of immunoinformatics, protein-DNA interactions, and disease research.

Research Focus

Transcription Factor Analysis

Studying transcription factor effector domains and protein-DNA interactions to understand gene regulation mechanisms.

Vaccine Design

Developing immunoinformatic approaches for vaccine design against pathogens like SARS-CoV-2 and Clostridium perfringens.

Computational Tool Development

Creating interactive web applications and computational pipelines for biological data analysis.

Disease Biomarkers

Identifying and validating biomarkers for diseases including COVID-19, diabetes, and liver cancer.
